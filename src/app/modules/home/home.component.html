Our story

✻

In 1970, the Rana family brought tailoring to Ludhiana, Punjab. The combination of hand-selected fabrics and hand-sewn tailoring allowed discerning customers to stand out from the crowd. For those who know what it means to be seen, Rana’s has become the go-to source to achieve custom looks that complement their personas.

Rana’s association with the tailors in Ludhiana dates back before 1975; this is a legacy and heritage that dates back to three generations. The Rana family sources fabrics from the oldest mills in the world and exclusive sources. They have known the tailors in Ludhiana for more than a half a century. The way their garments drape your frame will make you appreciate the quality and construction that goes into every weave and every stitch.
